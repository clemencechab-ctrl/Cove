Our Story

A vision, a passion, a movement

2024

The Beginning

COVE was born from a frustration. The inability to find clothes that combine premium quality, authentic style and accessible prices. Clemence, founder of the brand, passionate about urban fashion since her teenage years, decided to create what she couldn't find anywhere else.

The name COVE evokes a refuge, a protected space - like a hidden cove where you can be yourself. That's exactly what we want to offer: clothes in which you feel like you, without compromise.

Vision

Our Philosophy

At COVE, we believe that streetwear is not just a fashion - it's a culture, a way of expressing yourself. Each piece is designed to be worn, lived, loved. Not to stay in a closet.

We refuse fast fashion and its derivatives. Our collections are limited, carefully designed, produced in small quantities. Less but better.

Quality

Our Commitment

Thick cotton, reinforced seams, careful finishing. We work with European workshops that share our values. Each garment is designed to last years, not seasons.

Fair price is our promise. No excessive margins, no unnecessary intermediaries. Directly from us to you.

Future

What's Next?

COVE is growing, but stays true to its origins. An independent brand, created by passion, supported by a community that shares the same values.
